About Me

Hi, my name is Namrata and I love to write!!

I write about life and my approach to live a good life. I write on my failures, my mistakes and my successes; I write on my hopes, wishes and dreams. My stories might not be mind-blowing or super inspiring, but I hope that you can always find a bit of yourself in it. It’s just a reminder to “BREATHE”.

This blog is about creating positive change in your life, to find ways to make life easier and more enjoyable.

Let’s worry less, live in the moment more and allow ourselves to bask in the moments of happiness instead of always feeling overwhelmed by everything we juggle on a daily basis!!
